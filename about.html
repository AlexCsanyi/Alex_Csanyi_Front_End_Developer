About me.


I am a Front End Developer with a track record of being a self-starter who learns new things quickly, with a passion for the end user experience, and an intellectual curiosity to learn new tools and technology. I enjoy solving problems. Figuring out how to best implement a design, fixing bugs that crop up, making front end code work with the back end code. For me, development is all about creative problem solving.

On a team, I tend to help bring out a clearer sense of purpose and encourage clearer communication, resulting in better team results and a higher sense of satisfaction for all involved.
I really value the idea to not only focus on developing my own web development skills but also help others improve their craft, because I’ve gained so much from others sharing their skills and knowledge.
This is why I am working on adding a blog section to my portfolio site and share my experience through posts and build a library of resources I used and use to learn.

"The key to being a successful programmer is to learn how to learn" - Shannon Burnst
