A Front End Developer's Journey

"Don't let life discourage you; everyone who got where he is had to begin where he was"
Richard L. Evans

My goal with the blog is to share the resources I use / used to learn the fundamentals of front end development and programming.
The posts mainly focus on HTML, CSS, JS and React (tips, tutorials, best practices); but some posts are here to share my experience with interviews, the learning material I used to prepare and to give you inspiration to overcome the difficulties self-taught developers have to face.
I hope you find at least some of my content useful and if you have any questions please feel free to reach out.
